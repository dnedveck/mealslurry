mealslurry

An extensible framework for eating, optimized for:

  • time
  • nutrition
  • mental energy
  • cost

This is done through:

  • using a spreadsheet to determine the nutrients to take in per week
  • cooking all ingredients together 1x/week
  • blending all ingredients together (immersion blenders are great)
  • partitioning out mealslurry into Mason jars (or equivalent low-cost food storage option)
  • eating mealslurry 3x/day

For example: I spend ~$60/wk to eat 2200 kcal/day, 3 bunches of kale + broccoli, 120g protein per day (primarily from grass-fed beef), and it only takes me 1h30min to prepare it every Sunday. Link to spreadsheet here, currently a ketogenic version. I purposely create 18/21 weekly meals so that I have three meals to eat other food with. It helps with compliance.

Since one decides on the food that goes into it, it can meet most dietary constraints.
